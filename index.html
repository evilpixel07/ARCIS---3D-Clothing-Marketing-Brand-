02 / PHILOSOPHY

We make clothing to be worn and lived in.

Our pieces are made in small batches, using single-source fabrics that soften and take shape over time.

This collection focuses on natural fibers, structured cuts, and details that serve a purpose.

01 — THE SEAMS

Finished with bound French seams to prevent fraying. Constructed using high-density cotton thread for longevity.

02 — THE LINEN

Sourced from a historic mill in Nishiwaki, Japan. A heavy 240g linen, pre-washed for a soft drape and textured surface.

03 — THE PATTERN

Cut and assembled by hand in Osaka. Designed with a relaxed shoulder and deep pockets that sit naturally at the hip.

03 / MATERIAL
